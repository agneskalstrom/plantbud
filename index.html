What is PlantBud?

PlantBud is an online community and marketplace for house plant enthusiasts looking to donate, swap and adopt plants in their area.

We believe plants are friends and should be treated with the respect they deserve. Don’t throw your healthy house plants away – let another PlantBud adopt them!

Agnes Kalström, plant lover and cat-mother-to-be, founded PlantBud in late 2020 as part of the module Design for the User at Hyper Island in Stockholm.
